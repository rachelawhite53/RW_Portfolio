Resources Utilized During this Project

This project draws inspiration from both experimental and computational research on swirling jets, as well as modern web visualization frameworks.

Design Inspiration

  • Billant, P., Chomaz, J.-M., & Huerre, P. (1998). Experimental observations of vortex breakdown structures. Journal of Fluid Mechanics. — Guided the general flow loop design and swirling jet principles.
  • Oberleithner, K. et al. (2011–2020). Research on Precessing Vortex Cores (PVC) and global instability in turbulent swirling jets. — Provided swirler inspiration and other insights.

Additional Sources

  • Douglas, E. & Lesshafft, L. (2022). Confinement effects and bistability in laminar swirling jets.
  • Liang, K. H. & Maxworthy, T. (2005, 2008). Instability modes and ambient effects in plug-flow swirling jets.
  • Gallaire, F., Rott, S., & Chomaz, J.-M. (2004). Forced oscillations and instability response in swirling jets.
